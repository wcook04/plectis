## Supersession Semantics

Use these labels when future agents choose pattern material:

- **Parent**: the deeper organ row. Select this when constructing a future leaf.
- **Fold**: keep as child evidence, fixture stage, view, or receipt under a parent. Do not select as a standalone leaf.
- **Supersede**: a narrower or older row should route to the richer row for selection.
- **Sibling prerequisite**: do not fold; carry alongside the parent because it is required substrate.
- **Reframe**: keep the generic mechanism but drop release, dissemination, or live-private packaging.
- **Macro-only / private-fixture-only**: useful internally, but public or portable projection requires synthetic state.

Supersession here means selection posture, not deletion. Provenance remains in the append-only ledger.
