# Axioms

1. **Patterns Are Primitives Before Projections.** A projected artifact must bind to typed pattern identity, source posture, fixture status, validator coverage, and anti-claim before it claims public meaning.
2. **Routes Precede Search.** A cold agent starts from an entry packet, option surface, or typed route before widening into source files.
3. **Standards Are Executable Grammar.** A standard is not a style note; it is a validator-ready contract for a typed kind.
4. **Status Authority Cannot Upgrade Silently.** Advisory, evidence-only, generated, synthetic, and source-authority states stay distinct until a receipt records the upgrade.
5. **Source Coupling And Omission Are Structural.** Compressed projections must name their source coupling and what they omit.
6. **Claims Strengthen Only Through Receipts.** Validators, tests, proof cells, negative cases, or explicit boundaries are the only path from assertion to stronger status.
7. **Durable Work Captures Before Prose.** A blocker, residual, or side finding becomes durable before it becomes narrative.
8. **Work Lands Through Mission Transactions.** Mutations need bounded ownership, validation, receipts, and closeout state.
9. **Compression Is A Source-Derived Choice Surface.** A shorter view is valid only when it preserves drilldown handles back to source contracts.
10. **The Public/Private Boundary Is Structural.** Privacy posture belongs in manifests, source capsules, fixture policy, and scan receipts.
11. **Private-Risk Projection Requires Synthetic Fixtures.** A public example that stands for private-sensitive material must be synthetic, redacted, or schema-only.
12. **Local Lessons Propagate Upward.** Each organ landing records a reusable lesson or an explicit `nothing_to_refine` receipt.
