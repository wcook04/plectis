## Authority Boundary

This skill governs bounded belief-state process reward replay repair only. It cannot widen the authority ceiling, turn synthetic fixtures into live training evidence, or treat receipts as benchmark proof.

Anti-claims:

- Correcting an instance edge is not proof that process reward coverage is complete.
- Negative-case coverage is not propagation proof.
- Projection parity does not authorize live training or release claims.
