## Authority Boundary

This skill source governs public runtime organ standard authoring only. It can author a standard contract from public-safe fixtures, source-module manifests, validator refs, paper-module capsules, mechanism refs, and receipt expectations; it does not prove the organ works, authorize release, expose private bodies, call providers, mutate live repositories, or certify all standards that share the triad.

Anti-claims:

- Resolving this shared triad improves route coverage for ten standards; it is not proof that those standards are complete or runtime-correct.
- Generated health, graph, and coverage output are projections, not source evidence for standard truth.
- Body-floor imports, receipt existence, and validator refs must keep their authority ceilings; they cannot become release or private-equivalence claims.
