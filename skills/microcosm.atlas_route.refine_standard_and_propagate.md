## Authority Boundary

This skill governs class-level Atlas-route propagation only. It does not authorize source mutation, public release, private evidence access, provider calls, or whole-system correctness.

Anti-claims:

- Standard propagation cannot turn route availability into source authority.
- Generated health improvement is not proof that navigation is complete.
- Source-module and receipt evidence must keep its stated claim ceiling.
