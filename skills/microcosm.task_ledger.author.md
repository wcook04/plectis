## Authority Boundary

This skill source governs task-ledger admission only. A task-ledger record can make a capture, residual, dependency, or execution state durable; it does not prove the work is complete, public-safe, release-ready, or visible in every projection until the owning rebuild and checks show it.

Anti-claims:

- A markdown todo list, chat note, or generated view is not task-ledger authority.
- Live private ledger rows and provider/operator content must not be copied into public task-ledger records.
- Task-ledger triad resolution must not weaken std_microcosm_task_ledger or turn capture presence into execution proof.
