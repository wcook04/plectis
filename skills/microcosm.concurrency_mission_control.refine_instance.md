## Authority Boundary

This skill governs bounded concurrency mission-control instance repair only. It cannot widen the authority ceiling into live scheduling, host mutation, provider calls, release authority, or private equivalence.

Anti-claims:

- Corrected topology classification is not proof of live coordination closure.
- Body-free receipts do not prove scheduler behavior.
- Projection health must not hide active concurrency residuals.
