## Authority Boundary

This skill source governs bounded concept-instance correction only. It does not authorize source-of-record migration, new concept creation, or broad neighbour wiring without source evidence.

Anti-claims:

- Count movement is not proof that every concept has principle, mechanism, and axiom neighbours.
- A paired mechanism role is not a governed mechanism edge until a source row names the mechanism id.
- Generated health can expose the gap, but source evidence or validator computation must close it.
