## Authority Boundary

This skill source governs receipt admission only. A receipt can support only the command, validator, artifact, and authority ceiling it records; it does not prove release readiness, private-data equivalence, source-body completeness, or whole-system correctness.

Anti-claims:

- A success badge, screenshot, or untyped status note is not a receipt.
- A receipt does not upgrade authority beyond its declared evidence refs and omissions.
- Receipt triad resolution must not weaken std_microcosm_receipt or convert projection availability into source evidence.
