## Authority Boundary

This skill governs bounded pattern-assimilation instance repair only. It cannot widen the authority ceiling into global doctrine promotion, raw voice export, provider payload exposure, live ledger mutation, release, or whole-system correctness.

Anti-claims:

- Correcting owner-surface routing is not proof of global assimilation.
- Receipt existence does not prove propagation closure.
- Projection health must not hide active residuals.
