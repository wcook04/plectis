## Authority Boundary

This skill source governs standard propagation for concept work only. It does not make concept JSON parity, resolved triad edges, or graph reachability into proof that every concept has complete neighbours or implementation support.

Anti-claims:

- Do not weaken the concept standard to make health greener.
- Do not convert specimen presence into principle, mechanism, or axiom support.
- Do not close standard propagation without builder parity, validator checks, and scoped receipt binding.
