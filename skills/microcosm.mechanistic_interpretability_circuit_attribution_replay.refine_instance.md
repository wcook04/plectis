## Authority Boundary

This skill governs bounded circuit attribution replay repair only. It cannot widen the authority ceiling, turn public fixtures into model transparency evidence, or treat receipts as private-internal proof.

Anti-claims:

- Correcting an instance edge is not proof that attribution coverage is complete.
- Negative-case coverage is not propagation proof.
- Projection parity does not authorize interpretability or release claims.
