## Authority Boundary

This skill source governs bounded principle-instance correction only. It does not authorize source-of-record migration, new active principle creation, or broad downstream edge wiring.

Anti-claims:

- Count movement is not proof of downstream governance.
- A principle support row does not close anti-principle negation mapping.
- Generated health can expose the gap, but source evidence or validator computation must close it.
