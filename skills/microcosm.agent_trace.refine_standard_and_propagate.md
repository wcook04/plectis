## Authority Boundary

This skill governs class-level agent-trace propagation only. It does not certify private transcript contents, live session behavior, provider execution, release readiness, or whole-system correctness.

Anti-claims:

- Standard propagation cannot lower per-trace evidence requirements.
- Generated health improvement is not trace correctness proof.
- Source-module and receipt evidence must keep its stated claim ceiling.
