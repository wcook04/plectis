## Authority Boundary

This skill governs bounded authority-boundary instance repair only. It cannot widen authority, lower proof requirements, or treat diagnostics as release permission.

Anti-claims:

- Correcting a boundary is not permission to cross it.
- Negative-case coverage is not propagation proof.
- Projection parity does not authorize release or provider claims.
