## Authority Boundary

This skill source governs validator admission only. A validator can prove only the property it checks over the declared inputs; it does not prove release readiness, runtime correctness outside the check, source-body equivalence, or whole-artifact completeness.

Anti-claims:

- A checklist, broad review, or status count is not a validator without executable or bounded check semantics.
- Passing a validator is not propagation proof beyond its declared input contract and negative cases.
- Validator triad resolution must not weaken std_microcosm_validator or inflate release authority.
