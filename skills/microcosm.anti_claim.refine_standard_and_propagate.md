## Authority Boundary

This skill governs class-level anti-claim propagation only. It does not authorize release, provider calls, private-data equivalence, source mutation, score-based progress, or whole-system correctness.

Anti-claims:

- Standard propagation cannot convert denials into positive support.
- Generated health improvement is not proof of denied authority.
- Receipt evidence must keep its diagnostic claim ceiling.
