## Authority Boundary

This skill governs public Atlas-route authoring only. It does not authorize source mutation, public release, private evidence access, provider calls, or whole-system correctness.

Anti-claims:

- Route availability is not source authority.
- Affordance passports are navigation projections, not proof.
- Generated route graphs are not release evidence.
