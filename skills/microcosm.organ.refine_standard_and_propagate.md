## Authority Boundary

This skill source is for standard-level propagation, not ordinary organ repair. It preserves the no-laundering floor: a stronger standard is not evidence that every organ satisfies it until the validator and generated health prove the governed rows.

Anti-claims:

- Do not mark all organs governed by a new rule unless source evidence or typed residual routing supports each row.
- Do not flip generated corpus parity into source authority.
- Do not close a propagation lane without validator and projection freshness evidence.
