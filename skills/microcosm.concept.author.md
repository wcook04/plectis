## Authority Boundary

This skill source names the operating route for concept admission. Generated JSON parity and resolved standard triad edges are navigability evidence only; they do not prove concept coverage, principle implementation, mechanism instantiation, or source-of-record migration.

Anti-claims:

- A label or doctrine handle is not automatically a governed concept.
- A population specimen shows why the boundary matters, but it is not proof of complete concept neighbours.
- Generated graph pressure is not source evidence for `concept.implements_or_refines.principle`, `concept.instantiated_by.mechanism`, or `concept.abides_by.axiom`.
