## Authority Boundary

This skill governs bounded anti-claim instance repair only. It cannot turn a denial into positive evidence or lower proof requirements.

Anti-claims:

- Correcting anti-claim wording is not proof of the denied proposition.
- Negative-case coverage is not propagation proof.
- Projection parity does not authorize release or provider claims.
