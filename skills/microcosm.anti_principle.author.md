## Authority Boundary

This skill source names the operating route for anti-principle admission. Generated JSON parity and resolved standard triad edges are navigability evidence only; they do not prove failed-principle negation mapping or convert a rejection into positive support.

Anti-claims:

- A failed behavior is not automatically an anti-principle.
- An anti-principle guard edge is not positive evidence for an axiom or principle.
- `anti_principle.negates_failure_of.principle` remains residual until source evidence names the failed principle relation.
