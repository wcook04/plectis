## Authority Boundary

This skill governs class-level pattern-assimilation propagation only. It does not promote lessons outside owning lanes, export raw operator voice, expose provider payloads, mutate live Task Ledger rows, authorize release, or certify whole-system correctness.

Anti-claims:

- Standard propagation cannot turn local assimilation into global doctrine promotion.
- Generated health improvement is not propagation proof.
- Receipt existence cannot erase per-instance owner-surface or source gaps.
