## Authority Boundary

This skill governs bounded Batch 10 governance/compiler instance repair only. It cannot widen the authority ceiling into live ledger mutation, git operation authority, source mutation, provider dispatch, publication, release authority, or private equivalence.

Anti-claims:

- Corrected source refs are not live ledger authority.
- Body-free receipts do not prove release readiness.
- Projection health must not hide publication or source-mutation residuals.
