## Authority Boundary

This skill governs bounded sandbox policy replay repair only. It cannot widen the replay authority ceiling, turn public traces into live sandbox proof, or treat receipts as exploit-resistance evidence.

Anti-claims:

- Correcting an instance edge is not proof that the replay is complete.
- Negative-case coverage is not propagation proof.
- Projection parity does not authorize sandbox security or release claims.
