## Authority Boundary

This skill governs class-level authority-boundary propagation only. It does not authorize release, provider calls, private-data equivalence, complete secret detection, source mutation, score-based progress, or whole-system correctness.

Anti-claims:

- Standard propagation cannot widen authority by projection.
- Generated health improvement is not proof of release readiness.
- Receipt evidence must keep its diagnostic claim ceiling.
