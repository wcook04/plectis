# Anti-Principles

- Do not let external egress goals define the substrate root.
- Do not copy stale public foldering as architecture authority.
- Do not make one folder per pattern row.
- Do not turn every contract into a separate entry bureaucracy.
- Do not treat markdown claims as proof of workingness.
- Do not hide a missing validator behind a friendly skip.
- Do not let generated projections claim source authority.
- Do not use private or sensitive source bodies as public fixtures.
- Do not let a count or dashboard state substitute for an actionable item set.
