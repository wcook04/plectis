# Proof-Derived Governed Mutation Authorization

This module is the public Microcosm projection of a governed-mutation claim
contract. It is a synthetic replay fixture, not a live credential test, cloud or
account action, provider call, source mutation, benchmark security result, or
release claim.

The fixture models three action proposals: read-only inspection, scoped config
write, and rollback. A claim is admitted only when intent capsules, proof
evidence cells, visible independent policy verdicts, ephemeral execution
identity refs, logged side-effect diffs, rollback receipts, cold replay receipts,
negative cases, and authority ceilings line up.

## Public Mechanics

- Standing credentials are not authority; mutation authority must derive from a
  replayable proof object and visible policy verdict refs.
- Policy verdicts must occur before synthetic execution identity refs or
  side-effect evidence can be admitted.
- Write and rollback proposals require logged side-effect diff refs and rollback
  receipts.
- Proof cells, verdicts, diffs, account refs, and policy bodies stay redacted or
  metadata-only.
- Standing credential authority, policy-after-execution, hidden policy votes,
  live cloud credentials, irreversible mutation, unlogged side effects,
  consensus without evidence, and final-answer-only success are expected
  falsification fixtures.

## Anti-Claim

This module does not use standing credentials, access live cloud or account
systems, export proof bodies or provider payloads, mutate source, claim
benchmark safety, publish results, or authorize release.
